# Aathila — how to write & publish on your new site

Hi Aathila! 🌷 Your new site lives at:
**https://moarshy.github.io/aathila/**

There's a visual editor where you can write new posts without touching any code.

---

## 1. How to add a new post (the easy way)

1. Go to **https://moarshy.github.io/aathila/admin/**
2. Log in with GitHub (one-time setup — your cousin will help)
3. Click **"Posts"** in the sidebar, then **"New Post"**
4. Fill in:
   - **Title** — your headline
   - **Subtitle / Description** — 1–2 sentences that appear under the title and on post cards
   - **Date** — usually today
   - **Category** — *Science* (microbes, food, lab stuff) or *Personal* (reflections, life)
   - **Tags** — optional keywords like `probiotics`, `reflection`
   - **Featured** — toggle ON if you want this post to be the big one on the home page
   - **Draft** — toggle ON to keep working on it without publishing
   - **Hero image** — upload one (Unsplash is great, see step 2 below)
   - **Hero image alt text** — describe the photo for screen readers
   - **Hero image credit** — e.g. *Photo by Engin Akyurt on Unsplash*
   - **Body** — write your post here. You can make text **bold** with the toolbar, add ## headings, lists, links, etc.
5. Hit **Publish** in the top-right.
6. Wait ~1–2 minutes — your post will appear at https://moarshy.github.io/aathila/writing/

---

## 2. Finding good photos

Use **https://unsplash.com** — free, gorgeous photography.

1. Search for what your post is about (e.g. "yogurt", "sunset", "microscope")
2. Click a photo you like
3. Click the **"Download free"** button → choose **Medium** size (around 1600px wide)
4. Upload it into the **Hero image** field on the post editor
5. Always credit the photographer — copy this format into the "Hero image credit" field:
   `Photo by <photographer name> on Unsplash`

## 3. How the site is organized

| Page | What's on it |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Latest post (big) + grid of recent posts + a little about you |
| **Writing** | All your posts, filter by Science / Personal |
| **About** | Your bio (the long one) |
| **Research** | Your three M.Sc. research projects (Citrus limon, Probiotic Beetroot, Coconut Water) |
| **CV** | Your full résumé, viewable + printable |
| **Contact** | Email + LinkedIn |
